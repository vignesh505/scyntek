About Us

Scyntek is a med-tech company dedicated to improving patient outcomes through innovative, non-invasive deep vein thrombosis (DVT) detection technology.

Our Technology

DVTect is our groundbreaking calf sleeve equipped with photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors that monitor blood flow in real time. This non-invasive, continuous monitoring solution ensures early detection and timely intervention, saving lives and reducing healthcare costs.

Market Impact

With over 7 million at-risk surgical procedures annually in the U.S., DVTect addresses a critical gap in healthcare. Our device offers a proactive, affordable solution, significantly reducing the burden on patients and providers alike.
